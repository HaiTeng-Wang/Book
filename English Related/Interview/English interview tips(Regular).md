### 压力管理 / 如何解决困难 / 遇到过什么难点、挑战

#### 1. Question: Tell me about a time you faced a **challenge/stressful** at work. How did you handle it? 

In a previous project, we had a newly developed component that needed to be urgently used in the main project. Although we had completed testing and automated integration of the component, but after integration, the main project crashed unexpectedly, so this is a critical situation.

And we have received report and we need to resolve it as soon as possible.
So, First, I open the main project to do some research for the issue: I reviewed the error logs and the versions of the dependent libraries and determined that it was caused by an inconsistency in sub-dependencies. Then, after identifying the root cause, I fixed it and documented a long-term fix for the team, ensuring that all sub-dependencies use the same latest version library.

This made me realize that when we have urgent problems, we always need to stay calm and solve it quickly with our own experience.

#### 2.If assigned with multiple tasks at the same time, how would you organize yourself to produce quality work under **tight deadlines**?

In my previous role, I was sometimes assigned multiple tasks, such as fixing a critical issue and delivering a new feature for a sprint demo within two weeks. To ensure high-quality completion of all tasks, I would first quickly review the bug description to see if I could quickly identify the root cause. If a quick fix wasn't possible, i will estimate[ˈaɪsˈtiˈmait] the time it will take to fix the bug. Then, I would hand the new feature off to another colleague or put it on hold. So, by clearly prioritizing[praɪˈɔrəˌtaɪzing] and estimating, I am confident that I can complete multiple tasks with high quality within tight deadlines.

#### 3.问题解决能力/怎么寻求帮助(Question: Describe a time you needed **assistance** to complete a task.)

During our daily development process, we often work on API-related features, while others focus on interface development. For interface-related development, we often need the help of a UI designer to complete the task. For example, we need to confirm the final UI style and how it will adapt to different screen sizes. So, before developing a requirement, we usually reconfirm the specific details of the stroy to ensure safe acceptance of the feature.

#### 4.团队合作/同事间部门沟通(Question:How do you ensure smooth **collaboration with other departments**?)

If there are special situations that require a long discussion, I will also organize meetings to discuss the specific details face to face.
